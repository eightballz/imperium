MAINTAIN RESPECT

All players must maintain respect for each other. Harassment or hate speech will result in permanent bans. Political conflict is encouraged but keep it in-character.

BE APPROPRIATE

Keep all content and discussions appropriate for all ages. This includes builds, chat messages, and any shared media.

Destruction of property without consent is strictly prohibited. Protected builds are marked and any violations will be punished by server administration.
